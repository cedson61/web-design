5 Day Cancun Package:

  This modern, all-inclusive, resort overlooks the ocean, and features a selection of restaurants and bars, an expansive oceanfront pool, and a small spa with a hydrotherapy circuit.


All inclusive resort

Unlimited gourmet cuisine, wine and premium spirits
24-hour room service
Wireless internet access
Poolside concierge service

Places you will visit:

  • Cancún Underwater Museum: Modern underwater sculpture museum experienced via glass-bottom boat, snorkeling or scuba diving.

  • Isla Contoy: Small, uninhabited island with secluded beaches & a prime nesting site for sea birds & turtles.

  • Parque Garrafón: Natural reef park with snorkeling, kayaking & zip lining, plus a bar, swimming pool & lounge chairs.

  • Ventura Park Cancun: Lots of attractions, including a water park, zip line, dolphin encounter, classic rides & go-karts.
